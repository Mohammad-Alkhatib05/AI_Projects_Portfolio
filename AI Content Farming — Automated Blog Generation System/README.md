# AI Content Farming — Automated Blog Generation System

An AI-powered content automation workflow that automatically discovers trending topics, generates SEO optimized blog articles, creates AI generated images, and publishes them directly to WordPress.

This workflow demonstrates how AI agents, language models, and automation pipelines can create a fully autonomous content production system.

---

# Project Overview

This automation transforms trending news into fully written blog articles automatically.

The system:

• Collects trending articles from curated RSS feeds  
• Filters relevant AI-related topics using AI classification  
• Researches articles and extracts structured insights  
• Generates SEO optimized blog titles  
• Creates a structured blog outline  
• Writes a complete long-form article  
• Generates SEO metadata automatically  
• Creates a featured image using AI  
• Publishes the article to WordPress automatically  

This allows users to run a **fully automated AI powered blogging system**.

# How The Workflow Works

The automation is divided into multiple stages.

---

# 1. Collect Trending News

The workflow fetches articles from curated RSS feeds related to AI and technology.

Sources may include:

• TechCrunch  
• Wired  
• MIT Technology Review  
• HackerNoon  
• AI News  
• MarkTechPost  

Articles are collected with metadata including:

• title  
• summary  
• publication date  
• categories  
• source link  

Only recent articles are processed.

---

# 2. AI Relevance Filtering

Each article is analyzed using an AI classifier.

The system determines whether the article is relevant to AI related topics such as:

• AI model releases  
• research breakthroughs  
• AI startups  
• developer tools  
• regulation and policy  

Irrelevant content is discarded automatically.

---

# 3. Article Research and Knowledge Extraction

The workflow retrieves the full content of selected articles and extracts important information.

The AI analyzes the article and identifies:

• key ideas  
• companies and technologies mentioned  
• statistics or quotes  
• summarized insights  

This information becomes the knowledge base used for generating blog content.

---

# 4. Generate SEO Blog Title Ideas

The system generates multiple long-tail blog title ideas designed for search engines.

Each title is optimized for:

• keyword relevance  
• search intent  
• curiosity and click potential  

The titles are evaluated and the best performing title is selected automatically.

---

# 5. Generate Blog Outline

Once a title is selected, the system creates a structured article outline optimized for SEO.

Typical structure:

Introduction  
Background context  
Current trend analysis  
Key insights  
Future predictions  
Conclusion

This structure ensures articles are well organized and search engine friendly.

---

# 6. Generate Full Blog Article

The outline is expanded into a full article using a large language model.

The generated article includes:

• natural keyword usage  
• examples and explanations  
• citations from source articles  
• analysis and future insights  

Articles typically range from **1000–1500 words**.

---

# 7. SEO Optimization

After generating the article, the system automatically creates SEO metadata.

This includes:

• Meta title  
• Meta description  
• Image alt text  
• SEO friendly URL slug  

The workflow also performs a basic SEO analysis including:

• keyword density estimation  
• passive voice detection  
• readability score

---

# 8. AI Image Generation

A featured image is generated automatically using an AI image generation model.

The image prompt is derived from:

• blog title  
• article topic  
• emotional narrative context  

The generated image is downloaded and prepared for publishing.

---

# 9. WordPress Publishing

The workflow publishes the article directly to WordPress.

The process includes:

1. Converting Markdown to HTML  
2. Uploading the generated image  
3. Setting the featured image  
4. Applying SEO metadata  
5. Publishing the article automatically
